Containment Theorem

When you bet turn with a range full of semi-bluffs and the river bricks, you will be in a neutral-unfavourable spot with a range full of air. To refrain from overbluffing you must give up with a large quantity of your air. Such restraint is very difficult for a human.

Range Origin Theorem

The final result of a range is often more strongly dependant on its furthest back ancestor range than on any of the steps in between.

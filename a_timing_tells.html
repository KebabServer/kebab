Fold to 3Bet% Based on RFI Timing

Based on: Saulo Costa timing tell video.

RFI Timing Fold to 3Bet%
0-1.54s 47%
1.55s-9s 49%
9.01s+ 55%

Study conducted at $2/$5NL 6max Zoom

Observations:

1. The quickest RFIs are likely to receive fewer folds to 3bets in comparison to the overall fold percentage.

The reason is, if a player is multi-tabling and they pick up a premium hand with the chance to RFI, they will do so quickly in order to move to the next table and decision.

2. Players who take a very long time to RFI are likely distracted with other tables and are unsure if they want to RFI at another when holding a marginal hand.

This also ties in with our first theory, that a distracted player will instantly raise a strong hand. Therefore, these long RFI timings will result in a higher fold to 3bet percentage.

3. Also 5Bet Frequency goes up when the player has 3bet quickly. (Source data from Meta Game private training tool.)

3Bet Timing Fold to 4Bet%
0-4.09s 35%
4.10s-10.08s 42%
10.09s+ 45%

Study conducted at $2/$5NL 6max Zoom

Observations:

1. The quickest 3Bets are likely to receive fewer folds to 4Bets in comparison to the overall fold percentage.

If a player is multi-tabling and they pick up a premium hand with the chance to 3Bet, they will do so quickly in order to move to the next table and decision.

2. Longer 3Bets are likely to receive more folds to 4Bets in comparison to the overall fold percentage.

Players who take a long time to 3Bet are likely debating whether or not they should 3Bet a marginal hand. They are checking things like their opponent's fold to 3Bet%, other various HUD stats, and current game flow dynamics.

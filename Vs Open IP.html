Where is the EV coming from in GTO solution?

Let's look at the UTG vs MP situation.

We are risking 7.5bb to win 4bb. We get cold 4bet by CO (3.3%), BTN (3.4%), SB (3.2%) and BB (3.4%). In total we face cold 4bet (1-3.3%)(1-3.4%)(1-3.2%)(1-3.4%)=12.7%. In addition UTG will 4bet us 20.3% and call us 27.4%. In total we face 4bet 1-(1-12.7%)(1-20.3%)=30.4%, call (1-12.7%)(27.4%)=23.9% and fold 45.7%

With a zero-EV hand the EV-equation is

-7.5bb + 45.7% × (7.5bb+4.5bb) + 23.9% × (7.5bb + 1.5bb + 7.5bb) × S = 0,

where S is the pot share of our hand.

Solving the equation for S. We get S = 56.9%

This means we need to win slightly over half the pot each time we get called.

Summary:Around 2/3rds of our EV comes from getting folds preflop and rest from postflop. This means that our EV goes up if we face less 4bets or calls preflop, or have a postflop edge. Respectively our EV goes down if we face more 4bets or calls, or play poorly postflop.

River Calldown Process

River Call Down Theorem

Bluffcatching only makes money when villain is overbluffing. There are three scenarios on the river.

  • If villain is clearly overbluffing, every bluffcatcher is +EV and should be called.
  • Conversely, if villain is underbluffing, every bluffcatcher is -EV and should be folded.
  • When villain is bluffing at optimal frequency, bluffcatcher with good blockers is +EV and should be called.

Calldown Process Algorithm

  1. Use bet sizing to find villain's optimal Value/Bluff ratio. (GTO)
  2. Count villain value combos to find out optimal bluff combos. (GTO)
  3. Call/fold by estimating if he is under/over bluffing. (Exploit)

Things to increase accuracy of the decision:

  • Population tendencies
  • Player reads
  • HUD stats
  • Board texture
  • Timing tells
  • Etc.
